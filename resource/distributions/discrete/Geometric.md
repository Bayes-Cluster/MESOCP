$$\text{Geometric Distribution: } X \sim Geom(p)$$

* PDF and Support: $f(k) = p (1 - p)^{k - 1}, \; k \in \{1, 2, \ldots\}$
* CDF: $F(k) = 1 - (1 - p)^{k}$
* parameter: $p \ in (0, 1]$, the success parameter
* mean: $\frac{1}{p}$
* Variance: $\frac{1-p}{p^2}$
