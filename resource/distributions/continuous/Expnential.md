$\text{Exponential Distribution: } X~Expo(\lambda)$

* PDF and Support: $f(x) = \lambda e^{- \lambda x} \ x\in (0, +\infty)$
* CDF: $F(x) = 1 - e^{-lambda x}$
* parameter: $\lambda \in (0, + \infty)$, rate
* mean: $\frac{1}{\lambda}$
* Variance: $\frac{1}{\lambda^2}$
